About

My name is Sam. For as long as I can remember I have always wanted to help those around me. As a result, my work experience includes being a member of the US Air Force as an aircraft mechanic, IT support for a K-8 school, and now creating apps to help those in the Corrections system. I love technology and all things nerd culture.

I am currently learning Bootstrap, HTML 5, CSS 3, along with Javascript. This website is the result of my studying and courses I have taken so far. I am also working on a Discord event-scheduling bot for my friends who live all across America and the world as a small hobby project!

My current career goals are to further my knowledge in mobile development so I can build apps that are intuitive, cutting-edge, and enjoyable for the user. I have experience with C#, SQL, and server-side Java, with some Python and C++ from personal projects and college. I am currently working with the newest version of Bootstrap and Javascript to incorporate web-based forms into the apps I create. These apps make RESTful calls to web services and other neat features like drawing routes on maps, taking and cropping pictures, as well as using NFC technology.

Outside of my career I enjoy video games, such as Destiny 2, sports, and recently have been travelling more. I am also getting comfortable cooking in the kitchen. I really enjoy quiet weekends spent on the couch watching Stranger Things, any of the Marvel or Star Wars series, and older shows like Scrubs, Boy Meets World, Stargate, and Community.
